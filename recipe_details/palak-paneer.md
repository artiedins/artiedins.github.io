# palak paneer

## Ingredients

- oil
- butter
- cumin
- bay leaf
- garlic
- serrano chili
- ginger
- red onion
- salt
- tomatoes
- tomato paste
- coriander
- tumeric
- chili powder
- spinach
- mustard greens
- garam masala
- yogurt

## Equipment

- box grater
- vegetable peeler

## Notes

- try grating the ginger and tomates, and shaving the red onion
- cook garlic, onion, and tomato, then blanch and puree greens, and combine
- for chili powder, use gochugaru or kashimiri
- remove serrano chili and bay leaf
